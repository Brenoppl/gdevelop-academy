# GDevelop Academy Roadmap

## Development Phases and Timeline

### Phase 1: Research and Design (Q1 2026)
- Conduct thorough research on educational needs for game development.
- Design core curriculum and learning objectives.

### Phase 2: Content Creation (Q2 2026)
- Develop course materials, tutorials, and resources.
- Collaborate with educators and industry professionals for content validation.

### Phase 3: Platform Development (Q3 2026)
- Build the online platform for hosting courses.
- Implement interactive features like quizzes and forums.

### Phase 4: Testing and Feedback (Q4 2026)
- Launch a beta version of the platform.
- Gather user feedback for improvements.

### Phase 5: Official Launch (Q1 2027)
- Officially launch GDevelop Academy to the public.
- Begin promotional activities and outreach.

### Phase 6: Continuous Improvement (Ongoing)
- Regularly update course materials based on new developments in game design and technology.
- Engage with the community for feedback and suggestions.
