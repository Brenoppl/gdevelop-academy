# Comprehensive Library of Free Assets

## Images/Sprites
- **OpenGameArt** - A wide variety of free graphics and sprites for games.
- **Kenney.nl** - High-quality game assets including 2D and 3D graphics.

## Audio (Music and Sound Effects)
- **Freesound** - A collaborative database of Creative Commons Licensed sounds.
- **Incompetech** - Royalty-free music created by Kevin MacLeod.

## Creation Tools
- **GIMP** - Free and open-source image editor suitable for creating game assets.
- **Audacity** - Free, open-source, cross-platform audio software.

## Common Licenses
- **Creative Commons License** - Understand the different types of licenses, such as CC0, CC BY, etc. that allow you to use and share assets.
- **Public Domain** - Assets that can be used without restriction.

## Recommendations by Game Genre
- **Platformers** - Look for sprite sheets and tile sets that fit the 2D aesthetic.
- **Puzzle Games** - Check out assets that emphasize clean graphics and user interface elements.

## Important Tips
- Always read the license agreements for each asset before use.
- Give proper credit to creators when required by the license.

## Recommended Packages
- **Unity Asset Store** - A selection of free and paid assets for Unity developers.
- **Godot Asset Library** - A repository of assets specifically for Godot Engine users.

## Closing Notes
- The above resources are a starting point for finding free game assets. Ensure to stay updated with online communities for new releases and tools.
