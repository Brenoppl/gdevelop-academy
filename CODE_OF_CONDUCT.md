# Code of Conduct

## Our Commitment
We are committed to providing a friendly, safe, and inclusive environment for all members of our community. We believe that diversity in our community enhances the learning experience and fosters collaboration among members.

## Standards
To achieve a safe and inclusive community, we expect all participants to:
- Be respectful and considerate of others.
- Communicate openly and helpfully.
- Encourage and support fellow community members.
- Be responsible and accountable for your actions.
- Embrace diversity in all forms.

## Enforcement
If a community member fails to comply with these standards, we may take action, which could include:
- A warning to the member.
- Temporary or permanent expulsion from the community.
- Reporting the member to the appropriate authorities if necessary.

## Reporting Procedures
If you witness or experience unacceptable behavior, please report it to the community leaders directly. Reports can also be made anonymously if preferred. We take all reports seriously and will investigate each incident thoroughly. Our priority is to safeguard our community's values and members.

Thank you for helping to create a safe and welcoming environment for everyone!
